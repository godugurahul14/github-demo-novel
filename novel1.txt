Arjun worked night shifts at the satellite station. Most nights were silent, filled with static and routine checks.

But tonight was different.

At exactly 2:17 AM, a signal appeared — sharp, repeating, and clearly not random. He leaned closer, heart racing.

It wasn’t from any known satellite.

“Hello?” he whispered, as if the universe could hear him.

The signal changed.

Three short pulses. Then one long.

Arjun froze. It felt like a reply.

For the first time in his life, silence no longer felt empty. It felt like something was waiting.
