Ravi was debugging late into the night. His screen flickered, and a strange message appeared:

“Error 404: Reality Not Found.”

He smirked. “Nice prank.”

But then things started disappearing.

First, the clock. Then the window. Then the door.

Panicking, he typed commands, trying to fix what made no sense.

“Restore system,” he entered.

The screen paused.

Then replied:

“Permission denied.”

Ravi realized something terrifying.

He wasn’t fixing the system.

He was inside it.
